Chicken Cury

Let's Discuss the Recipe of Sahi Paneer.....

Ingrediant:-

  1. Chicken
  2. Oil
  3. Tomato and Onion
  4. Ginger,Garlic and Green-Chili Paste
  5. Ground spices
  6. Chicken Masala

Recipe And Instruction

Prepare Ingredients:

  • Chop the tomatoes and onions finely.
  • Make the ginger-garlic-green chili paste if you haven't already.

Cook Onions and Paste:

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add the finely chopped onions and saute until golden brown then Add the ginger-garlic-green chili paste and cook until the raw smell disappears

Add Chicken:

  • Fry Chicken With Oninon and ginger,garlic,green-chili Paste for the time When chicken reduce their water. After this process fry Chicken for more 5 mint.

Add Tomatoes and Spices:

  • Add the tomatoes paste and cook for 5 minutes.
  • Add the ground spices: coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and Chicken Masala and salt to taste. Mix well and cook for another 5 minutes and the oil starts to separate.
  • Add hot water into the Chicken according to you, how much you need your chicken cury thik.And cook them into medium-to-low heat with cover for 15 mintues.

Finish and Serve:

  1. Garnish with coriander leaf and a sprinkle of garam masala if desired.
  2. Serve with hot naan, roti, or rice.

Enjoy your delicious Chicken Cury!
