Dal Chawal

Let's Discuss the Recipe of Dal Chawal.....

Ingrediant:-

  1. 1 cup Toor dal (pigeon peas) or Any other dal
  2. Oil
  3. Tomato and Onion
  4. Ginger,Garlic and Green-Chili Paste
  5. Ground spices
  6. Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Recipe And Instruction

Rinse and Cook the Dal:

  • Rinse the toor dal under cold water until the water runs clear.
  • In a pressure cooker or a pot, add the rinsed dal and 3 cups of water. Cook it until it’s soft and mushy (about 3-4 whistles in a pressure cooker or 25-30 minutes in a pot).

Prepare the Tadka (Tempering):

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, and dried red chilies (if using). Let them splutter.
  • Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until they become golden brown.
  • Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute or until the raw smell disappears.
  • Add the chopped tomatoes, green chilies, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the oil starts to separate from the mixture.

Combine and Simmer:

  • Add the cooked dal to the tempering mixture.
  • Stir well and let it simmer for about 5-10 minutes. Adjust the consistency by adding water if needed.
  • Taste and adjust salt or spices as necessary.

Garnish and Serve:

  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves if desired.
  • Serve hot with steamed rice or chapati.

Enjoy your delicious Dal Chawal!
