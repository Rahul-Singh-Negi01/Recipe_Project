Fish Fry

Let's Discuss the Recipe of Sahi Paneer.....

Ingrediant:-

  1. 500 grams fish fillets (like tilapia, cod, or your choice)
  2. 1/2 cup oil (for frying)
  3. 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  4. Ginger,Garlic and Green-Chili Paste
  5. Ground spices
  6. 1/2 cup rice flour or all-purpose flour
  7. Fresh coriander or lemon wedges for garnish

Recipe And Instruction

Marinate the Fish:

  • In a bowl, combine lemon juice, turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, ginger-garlic paste, and salt.
  • Coat the fish fillets with this marinade and let it sit for at least 30 minutes. For best results, refrigerate for a few hours.

Prepare for Frying:

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Dredge the marinated fish fillets in rice flour or all-purpose flour. Shake off excess flour.

Fry the Fish:

  • Place the fish fillets in the hot oil. Fry until golden brown and crispy on both sides, about 3-4 minutes per side, depending on the thickness of the fillets.
    • Remove the fish and drain on paper towels.

Serve:

  • Garnish with fresh coriander or lemon wedges.
  • Serve hot with a side of rice or a fresh salad.

Enjoy your delicious Fish Fry!
