Sahi Paneer

Let's Discuss the Recipe of Sahi Paneer.....

Ingrediant:-

  1. Paneer
  2. Oil
  3. Tomato and Onion
  4. Ginger,Garlic and Green-Chili Paste
  5. Ground spices
  6. Creem or Cashew Paste

Recipe And Instruction

Prepare Ingredients:

  • Chop the tomatoes and onions finely.
  • Make the ginger-garlic-green chili paste if you haven't already.
  • If using cashew paste, soak about 10-12 cashews in warm water for 15-20 minutes and then blend into a smooth paste.

Cook Onions and Paste:

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add the finely chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown then Add the ginger-garlic-green chili paste and cook until the raw smell disappears

Add Tomatoes and Spices:

  • Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and the oil starts to separate from the mixture.
  • Add the ground spices: coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt to taste. Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes.

Incorporate Cream or cashew Paste:

  • If using cream: Reduce the heat to low and stir in the cream. Cook for a couple of minutes.
  • If using cashew paste: Stir in the cashew paste and cook for 5 minutes.

Add Paneer:

  • Gently add the paneer cubes to the gravy and Stir carefully.
  • Simmer for 5-7 minutes on low heat.

Finish and Serve:

  1. Garnish with a little extra cream or a sprinkle of garam masala if desired.
  2. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.

Enjoy your delicious and creamy Sahi Paneer!
