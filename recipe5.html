Let's Discuss the Recipe of Sahi Paneer.....

Ingrediant:-

  1. 1 liter milk
  2. 1 cup sugar
  3. 1/4 teaspoon saffron strands
  4. 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  5. 1 tablespoon lemon juice or vinegar
  6. 1/4 cup water
  7. 1 tablespoon chopped pistachios, almonds and chopped cashews
  8. 1 tablespoon rose water (optional)

For the Chena (Paneer):

  • 1 liter milk
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice or vinegar
  • 1/4 cup water

Recipe And Instruction

Make the Paneer (Chena):

  • Boil the milk in a pan. Once it starts boiling, add lemon juice or vinegar to curdle the milk. Stir gently until the milk separates into curds and whey.
  • Strain the curds using a muslin cloth or a fine sieve. Rinse the curds under cold water to remove the acidic taste.
  • Squeeze out excess water from the curds and let it drain for about 10 minutes. Knead the curds gently to make a smooth dough.

Shape the Paneer Balls:

  • Divide the dough into small balls and flatten them into discs.
  • In a large pan, bring water and sugar to a boil to make a sugar syrup.
  • Gently drop the paneer discs into the boiling syrup. Cook for 5-7 minutes until they become spongy and absorb the syrup.
  • Remove from the syrup and set aside.

Prepare the Milk Sauce:

  • Boil the milk in a pan and reduce it by half.
  • Dissolve saffron strands in a tablespoon of warm milk and add it to the boiling milk.
  • Add cardamom powder and sugar to taste. Mix well.

Combine and Chill:

  • Add the paneer discs to the milk sauce and simmer for about 10 minutes.
  • Let it cool and then refrigerate for a few hours to allow the flavors to meld.
  • Garnish with chopped nuts and rose water before serving.

Enjoy your delicious and creamy Ras Malai!
